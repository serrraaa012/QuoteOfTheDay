# Quote of the Day

> *"Words are, in my not-so-humble opinion, our most inexhaustible source of magic."* – J.K. Rowling

---

## About The App

**Quote of the Day** is a beautifully crafted Android application that delivers daily inspiration through carefully curated quotes. What makes this app truly special is that it features **original literary work** alongside timeless wisdom from legendary figures.

For years, I've been writing. Poetry, reflections, observations about life, love, and everything in between. Words have always been my sanctuary. This app is where my two greatest passions—technology and literature—finally meet.

---

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Daily Quotes** | 15+ handpicked quotes blending classics and original writing |
| **Save Favorites** | Build your personal collection of meaningful words |
| **Share Wisdom** | Spread inspiration with just one tap |
| **Elegant Dark Theme** | Premium feel with golden accents |
| **Personal Touch** | Original quotes from my literary journey |
| **Quote Refresh** | New inspiration with every tap |

---

## Original Quotes Featured

This app includes my own literary work alongside timeless classics:

> *"Love is what you give without any expectations."*

> *"Tough World, Rough Opinions."*

> *"Love is all about thorns and roses."*

> *"Some secrets are better kept secrets."*

> *"A person who never learns never earns."*

> *"It's better to be silent than being violent."*

> *"Humans tend to blame themselves when misfortune strikes their loved ones."*

I included my own voice because I believe that **inspiration shouldn't be limited to the famous**. Every perspective matters, and your words might be exactly what someone needs to hear.
